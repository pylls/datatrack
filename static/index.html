Welcome to the Data Track!

Using the tool is a three-step process:

First

Import your data from Google

You can download your data from Google and upload it to Data Track. Then it is time to see what you have disclosed to Google

Second

Wait while the Data Track parses your data

Have you ever noticed you have bunch of data on Google? To preserve your privacy your data is deleted from DataTrack database each time you close it

Third

Explore your data through the Data Track

Now it is the amazing time to explore your data through Data Track's different visualization modes


Let's get started!

Here we help you import your data from Google.

First Step


You have to log in to your Google account.

Second Step


Perfect! Now jump to "My Account" setting at the top right corner.

Third Step


Between available settings and tools select "Personal Info & Privacy"

Forth Step


Under the "Control Your Content" section, download your data and create archive.

Fifth Step


Among all Google products just select "Location History".

Sixth Step


Finally you just need to press "Create Archive" button to create zip file.
